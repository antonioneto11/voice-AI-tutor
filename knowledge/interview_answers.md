# Interview Preparation

## Product leadership framing
For leadership interviews, strong answers usually connect system design to business value, operational risk, and learning loops. Explain how you would define quality, measure it, monitor it, and improve it.

## Agentic throughput
A strong explanation of agentic throughput connects quality-adjusted output to time, cost, and human oversight. It is not just how fast the system moves. It is how much useful work gets done safely and reliably.

## Simple interview pattern
A useful interview structure is: define the concept, explain why it matters, describe how you would measure it, then give one concrete product example.
